About Simpl

Welcome to Simpl, your comprehensive health management platform. We believe that data and insights are the keys to revolutionizing healthcare.

Our approach is centered around leveraging health data, technology, top-level data security and AI to deliver solutions that empower providers, payers, and patients.

We go beyond traditional healthcare settings, engaging users before, during, and after appointments to provide comprehensive care. Whether you are a provider looking to streamline your workflow or a patient seeking control of your health, Simpl is here to revolutionize your healthcare experience.

Mission

Our mission is to empower healthcare consumers and providers by evolving proactive and predictable outcomes. We are committed to making healthcare proactive, predictable, and efficient for everyone.
