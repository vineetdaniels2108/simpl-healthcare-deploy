The Simpl Way

Many sources, one platform

Simpl streamlines your access to comprehensive medical records, insurance claims, wearables data, patient care team, clinical and lab results, imaging, and more - enabling you to securely and effortlessly access comprehensive healthcare data within one platform. Say goodbye to the hassle of navigating multiple systems, and embrace the future of healthcare management with Simpl.

AI-powered health management

Experience the future of healthcare with Simpl's AI-driven solutions. Access personalized health insights that go beyond data – understand your medical records through easy-to-grasp summaries. Our predictive analytics help you stay ahead of your health, while medication management becomes effortless with tailored reminders. Simpl simplifies health management, putting you in control.

Coordinate care, from anywhere

Say goodbye to hassle and hello to seamless care coordination. Simpl empowers both providers and patients to effortlessly schedule appointments, all while ensuring your insurance status is crystal clear. Connect with your care team securely using end-to-end encryption, making communication a breeze. Wherever you are, Simpl is your partner in coordinating care efficiently and securely.
